Ultrasound and Doppler Scanning

Scanned, read, and explained — in one visit

Pregnancy scans, pelvic ultrasound, and Doppler studies are done on site and read by our in-house radiologist, Dr. Karthik, MD Radiology. No carrying films to another centre, no waiting days for a report.

Because the radiologist and your doctor are under the same roof, findings are discussed together and explained to you the same visit — in Telugu, Hindi, or English.

In-house Laboratory

Tests done here, not sent across town

Samples are collected and processed on site, results go straight to your doctor, and you hear what they mean — not just receive an envelope.

✦

Biochemistry

Blood sugar and glucose tolerance, thyroid profile, liver and kidney function, lipids, and electrolytes — the routine panels pregnancy and women's health care depend on.
❋

Pathology and Haematology

Complete blood counts, haemoglobin, blood grouping, urine examination, and infection screening — processed in our own pathology lab.
✧

Antenatal Screening

The scheduled tests of pregnancy — first-trimester blood work, glucose challenge, and routine monitoring — timed to your checkup calendar so nothing is missed.

Why it matters

A checkup shouldn't turn into a week of errands

When the scan, the lab, and the doctor are in different places, every question takes three trips and a stack of reports. At Srishti, diagnostics sit under the same roof as your care — so a concern raised in the morning can often be tested, reviewed, and answered the same day.
