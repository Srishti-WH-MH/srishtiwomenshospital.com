Doctor-led care

The doctor who knows you shouldn't have to hand you over at the moment that matters

Care at Srishti is led by Dr. Ashwini, DNB Obstetrics & Gynaecology — and she is on duty round the clock. The doctor you consult at your first visit is the doctor who knows your history, answers your questions, and is there for the important moment.
