Maternity Services

From the first scan to the day you go home

Pregnancy is emotional, family-centered, and full of questions. Srishti supports you with regular consultations, scans, delivery planning, and hospital care — all in one place, all with the same doctor.

✦

24/7 Maternity and Emergency Delivery

Labour can start at 3pm or 3am. A doctor is on duty round the clock, and the delivery room and operation theater are always prepared. In labour or worried about the baby's movements? Come straight in — or call first.
❋

Normal Delivery and Caesarean Section

We support normal delivery with continuous fetal monitoring in a calm, private delivery room. When a caesarean is planned — or becomes necessary during labour — the modular operation theater is under the same roof, minutes away.
✧

High-Risk Pregnancy Care

Blood pressure, gestational diabetes, previous caesarean, twins, or age-related concerns — high-risk pregnancies get closer monitoring, more frequent scans, and a clear delivery plan, with the surgical ICU on site if it is ever needed.
✦

Antenatal Checkups and Delivery Planning

Scheduled checkups, growth scans, and blood work on a clear calendar — with time to ask questions. As your due date approaches, you and your family hear the delivery plan in plain words, in Telugu, Hindi, or English.

Your Journey

One doctor, one place, the whole way

  1. 01

    First visit and scan

    Confirm the pregnancy, meet Dr. Ashwini, and get your checkup calendar — ultrasound and blood work done the same visit.

  2. 02

    Regular checkups

    Growth scans, monitoring, and unhurried consultations. High-risk pregnancies get closer attention from the start.

  3. 03

    Delivery planning

    You and your family hear the plan clearly — normal delivery or caesarean, what to expect, and when to come in.

  4. 04

    Delivery and recovery

    Delivery with the doctor who knows you, then recovery in a private patient room with space for family.

Doctor-led maternity care

The doctor at your first checkup is the doctor at your delivery

Maternity care at Srishti is led by Dr. Ashwini, DNB Obstetrics & Gynaecology, on duty round the clock. No handovers to a stranger at the moment that matters — she knows your history, your scans, and your plan.

Every scan and report is explained the same visit, in Telugu, Hindi, or English — so you and your family leave with answers, not just papers.
