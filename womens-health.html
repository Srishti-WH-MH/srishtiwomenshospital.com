Women's and Gynaecology Services

A year-round clinic for every stage of a woman's health

Not every visit is about pregnancy. Srishti is a place women can bring any concern — routine or worrying — and have it heard, examined, and explained clearly.

Fibroids, Cysts, and Screening

Evaluation of fibroids and ovarian cysts, and routine screening — so small concerns are caught while they are small.
✧

Fertility Consultation

Trying to conceive? Start with an unhurried consultation, cycle evaluation, and scans — close to home, without judgement.
✦

Menopause and Wellness Care

Support through perimenopause and menopause — symptoms taken seriously, bone and heart health watched over time.

Diagnostics and Pathology Lab

Blood work and tests done on site, results explained by the doctor who ordered them — no second trip across town.

Ultrasound and Doppler Scanning

Scanned, read, and explained — in one visit

Pregnancy scans, pelvic ultrasound, and Doppler studies are done on site and read by our in-house radiologist, Dr. Karthik, MD Radiology. No carrying films to another centre, no waiting days for a report.

Because the radiologist and your doctor are under the same roof, findings are discussed together and explained to you the same visit — in Telugu, Hindi, or English.
